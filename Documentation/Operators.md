# Operators

## Math
* `+`: Addition, Unary Plus
* `-`: Subtraction, Unari Minus
* `*`: Multiplication
* `/`: Division
* `//`: Integer Division. Rounds towards negative-infinity.
* `%`: Modulus.
* `^`: Exponentiation.
* `..`: Concatenation.

## Bitwise Math
* `&`: Bitwise AND.
* `|`: Bitwise OR.
* `~`: Bitwise NOT, Bitwise XOR.
* `<<`: Bitwise Left Shift.
* `>>`: Bitwise Right Shift.

## Comparison
* `<`: Less Than.
* `>`: Greater Than.
* `<=`: Less Than or Equal To.
* `>=`: Greater Than or Equal To.
* `==`: Equal To.
* `!=`: Not Equal To.

## Logical
* `&&`: Logical AND.
* `||`: Logical OR.
* `and`: Logical AND.
* `or`: Logical OR.
